ADISAPTAGRAM | WEST BENGAL

2019

Abin Design Studio built the Waterfront Arena in rural West Bengal as a low-cost, community-driven clubhouse supporting young footballers. Utilizing local materials, labor, and donations.

The story of Adisaptagram's football club and the construction of a clubhouse by Abin Design Studio is a compelling narrative of community-driven development.

Utilizing local materials, labor, and donations, the structure includes basic sanitation, multipurpose spaces, and a viewing deck. It symbolizes inclusive development, sustainability, and hope for a better community future.
